Alone

Stranded on an island

Perched on the sand

All your eyes can see is the Water

Without my mother and father

On the face of the water you gaze at a fin

As sharp as the tip of a pin

Maybe it belongs to a shark

It's now getting dark

I'm alone without a single friend

When will it all end...?
